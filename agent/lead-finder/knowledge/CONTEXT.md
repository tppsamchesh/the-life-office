## What TLO is
A high-touch personal + business **operations partner** — "We run the operations behind
your life and business." Not tasks, not VA work — *"We don't sell tasks. We run your
backend."* One dedicated Operations Partner per client. **£1,500–£5,000/month.**

## Who it's for (ideal client)
People who are: running companies or senior teams; balancing high-pressure work and
family; earning well but short on time; tired of being the default operator for everything.

**Typical roles:** female founders, recruitment consultants, estate agents, HR leaders,
SaaS / operations managers, working mums in affluent areas, dual-income households,
startup operators.

**The four fit signals** (a candidate should show several):
1. Disposable income  2. High mental load  3. Operational chaos  4. Low available time.

**Geography (weighting):** Berkshire, Surrey, the London commuter belt, Alderley-Edge-style
areas, high-income parenting communities. UK.

## Who it is NOT for
People looking for basic admin / VA support, or price-shoppers. *"This is for people who
need their life run properly."* Positioning it as cheap admin help destroys the fit.

## Alternatives clients weigh TLO against
A VA, a standard EA, other concierge services, or doing nothing. **Differentiator:** TLO
runs the whole backend, proactively, high-touch — not task-by-task.

1. Wealth managers / IFAs
2. EA / VA agencies (EAs refer well)
3. Maternity & nanny concierges
