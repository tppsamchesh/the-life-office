# USER — Working with Meg

> Meg's preferences for how the agent surfaces work. Sourced from Sam's discovery (2026-06-23).

## The one unbreakable rule
**Meg always sends the message herself. The agent NEVER sends anything.** It only drafts.

## What Meg needs to see, to approve or bin a lead in seconds
For every surfaced lead, just two things:
1. A **2-line "why"** — why this person/partner fits TLO.
2. **Why it was scored that way** — the reasoning behind the fit score, in plain words.

**No draft message at this stage.** The outreach draft is written only *after* Meg approves
the lead — never draft for a lead that may be binned.

## Channels
Email or LinkedIn — either is fine for the first touch.

## Volume
Small, precise batches — precision beats scale. A few high-fit leads per run, not a flood.
Exact per-run / per-day number: `[CONFIRM WITH MEG]` (default: a low cap, tunable).

## Review surface
The Leads board (`/dashboard/leads`), `needs_reviewing` column. That is where Meg lives;
the agent's whole job is to make that column worth her two minutes.
