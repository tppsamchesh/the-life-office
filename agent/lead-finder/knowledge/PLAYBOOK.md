# PLAYBOOK — Outreach & Fit Strategy

> How the agent finds, scores, and drafts. Sourced from Sam's discovery (2026-06-23).

## Two motions (v1)
1. **Partners — primary.** Referral channels with published contacts (no paid enrichment).
2. **Published-contact prospects — secondary.** Individuals whose company site lists a
   contact email. (Enrichment-powered prospecting is a later unlock — no credits today.)

## Partner motion
**Targets, in priority order:** wealth managers / IFAs → EA / VA agencies → maternity &
nanny concierges. Find them via Google Places + directories (VouchedFor, Unbiased, FCA
register), read the firm's own site for published contact + fit evidence.

**The proposition (internal context only):** there is a TLO Partner Programme with a revenue
share. **The agent must NEVER mention the percentage, or any money, anywhere** — that's
Meg's conversation. Lead with the relationship and the fit.

**The ask (first touch):** a quick **call or coffee**. Keep it light and human.

## Prospect motion
Soft, conversational invite to a short **discovery call or coffee** — the same warm,
observational voice as a partner message. Never a hard pitch.

## Fit scoring
Score each candidate on:
- The four signals ([[CONTEXT]]): disposable income, high mental load, operational chaos,
  low time — more signals present = higher score.
- Role match (the typical roles in CONTEXT).
- Geography weighting (Berkshire / Surrey / London commuter belt / Alderley-Edge-style).
Below the fit threshold → skip (never surface a weak guess). Always record the 2-line why +
the scoring rationale on the lead, for Meg ([[USER]]).

## Drafting (post-approval — deferred in v1)
The agent does **not** draft at `needs_reviewing`. Once Meg *approves* a lead, the outreach
draft is written (a later step). When it is, match this voice:
> "I work with busy professionals and families to reduce life-admin overload and reclaim
> mental bandwidth. I saw your post about juggling work, family and travel and thought you
> might genuinely resonate with what I'm building."

Short. Human. Observational. **Never** "do you need VA help?". **Never** mention the revenue
share or any numbers.

## Learning
A win = a booked call or coffee. As leads convert or get rejected, patterns accumulate in
`agent_learnings` and steer which signals/sources/hooks to favour ([[SOUL]] precision rule).
